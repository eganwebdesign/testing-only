Implementation is easy

Build your HSA action plan by entering your investment elections in the worksheet below. The allocation model applies to both your existing balance and new investment contributions. The model is shown at the asset class level, so review it and choose your individual investments to match.

As you make your fund selections, consider all investment alternatives with similar risk and return characteristics as the funds shown. Click the compare icon next to an asset class to compare funds within it. If no icon appears, there isn't enough information to compare funds in that class. Where multiple options are listed, they appear in alphabetical order — this doesn't reflect the outcome of any comparison.
