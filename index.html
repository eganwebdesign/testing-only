Action plan

The system will help you determine an investment mix and savings level that may be appropriate for your goals, time horizon, and tolerance for risk. Before we can build your action plan, we need to know more about your risk level and your approach to investing.

Risk Tolerance

Your tolerance for risk is an important part of your HSA investment strategy. The system can help you determine your tolerance for risk with a short questionnaire, or you can enter your own risk level. Our risk scale ranges from 1 (most conservative) to 100 (most aggressive).
